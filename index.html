How it works

The team at Adso gathers deals for products that you can promote in your podcast through baked-in ad placement. We handle the promo code, your campaigning landing page, tracking of the traffic, earnings you generate and distribute it back to you. Creating an ad campaign will take less than a minute.

STEP 1

Generate a campaign

Find a product or service (of hundreds) you want to promote, and that you believe would be extra interesting for your audience.

Generate a campaign for the product you have chosen. You receives content and pitch material for your baked-in promotion. A unique landing page on Adso.io/YourPodcast/YourCampaign is generated with the proper tracking-link set-up by us.

STEP 2

Promote the campaign in your podcast

Read the baked-in ad in your podcast episode referring to your campaign site link and publish your episode.

STEP 3

Get your commission

Listeners go to the campaign link and buy the product/service you promote. We distribute the commission back to you. Follow all the traffic data and see your earnings on your Adso.io Dashboard.
